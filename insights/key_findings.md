# Key Business Findings & Recommendations

## Olist E-Commerce Analysis — 113,390 Orders (2016–2018)

---

## 1. Revenue Analysis

### Findings
- **Beauty & Health** is the #1 revenue category at R$1.27M — 9.33% of total platform revenue
- **Watches & Gifts** commands the highest average order value at R$200 per order — premium low-volume segment
- **Bed, Bath & Table** leads in order volume (11,649 orders) but ranks #3 in revenue — high volume, lower ticket size
- **November 2017** was the single biggest month at R$1.53M — clear Black Friday spike
- **September 2017** had the highest average order value at R$200 — customers buy less but spend more

### Recommendations
1. Prioritize Beauty & Health inventory and marketing spend ahead of Q4
2. Launch premium campaigns for Watches & Gifts targeting high-income segments
3. Introduce bundle deals for Bed, Bath & Table to increase average order value
4. Plan logistics capacity increases ahead of November every year

## 2. RFM Customer Segmentation

### Findings
| Segment | Customers | Avg Recency | Avg Spend | Total Revenue |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| Loyal Customers | 35,570 | 316 days | R$340 | R$12.29M |
| At Risk | 44,361 | 356 days | R$128 | R$5.68M |
| Champions | 1,938 | 263 days | R$587 | R$1.13M |
| Lost Customers | 10,206 | 555 days | R$59 | R$606K |

### Recommendations
1. **Champions (1,938 customers):** Launch VIP program — early access, free shipping, exclusive deals. Protect this segment at all costs.
2. **Loyal Customers (35,570):** Introduce loyalty points system to prevent migration to At Risk segment.
3. **At Risk (44,361):** Highest priority win-back campaign. A 10% recovery rate = R$568K additional revenue. Use urgency-based offers and personalized email outreach.
4. **Lost Customers (10,206):** Last-resort reactivation with heavy discount. If no response within 30 days, reallocate marketing budget to At Risk segment.

## 3. Delivery & Logistics

### Findings
| State | Orders | Avg Actual Days | Late Delivery % |
|-------|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| AL (Alagoas) | 437 | 24.5 | 21.3% |
| MA (Maranhão) | 808 | 21.7 | 18.2% |
| SE (Sergipe) | 384 | 21.4 | 15.9% |
| PI (Piauí) | 546 | 19.3 | 13.9% |
| CE (Ceará) | 1,482 | 20.6 | 13.1% |

- All states show **negative average delay** — packages arrive before estimated date on average
- Late deliveries are driven by **outlier orders** in northeastern states with weaker logistics infrastructure
- The platform is **systematically over-estimating** delivery times

### Recommendations
1. **Recalibrate delivery estimates** for AL, MA, SE, PI, CE — tighter estimates reduce perceived lateness without changing operations
2. **Partner with regional carriers** in northeastern states to reduce actual delivery times
3. **Flag high-risk orders** at checkout for northeastern states and proactively notify customers of potential delays
4. **Track delivery NPS by state** to measure improvement over time
